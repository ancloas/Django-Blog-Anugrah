About

Hi, I'm Anugrah Gupta, A software developer finding my way into the world of writing.

I'm new to the field of writing, but consider this place my playground, and I plan to enjoy, embracing my thoughts and ideas as I go

Why does this blog exist?

  • I want to share what I learn.
  • Create a community where we can figure things out together.
  • Explore the mysteries of our world without getting too philosophical.
  • Solve big problems together (or at least attempt to).
  • This is a dumpster where I will spill my thoughts, learnings, and plans.

What I write about?

  • Everyday life - the good, the bad, and the mundane.
  • Mental Health - because let's be real, it matters.
  • Common problems we all face and my unfiltered take on them.
  • Out-of-context topics that catch my interest: AI, universe, physics, atoms, brain science, chess, sports, etc.

What am I promising?

  • Authenticity: Don't want me to repeat what you have already read? I am not planning to, it's super boring.
  • Continuous learning: Expect fresh ideas and thoughts because I'm constantly on the hunt for answers, ready to share my discoveries with you.
  • Engagement: This is not a blog rather a bubble of all of our thoughts
  • Fun: Expect a sprinkle of humor and a light-hearted approach towards the serious stuff.
  • Stick-Figures: Because they are awesome and I like to draw.
  • Uncertainity: At this point, the direction of this blog is uncertain, so brace yourself for plenty of changes in every aspect.
  • Imperfection: Mistakes here, there, everywhere.

Stick around, and let's see where this takes us.
